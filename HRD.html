BRIEF NOTE ON HRD ACTIVITIES IN GVMC

The HRD Department commenced its activities from June, 2008, after conducting preliminary study of all the departments in relation to the HR in GVMC. The following activities are performed by the HRD wing:

  • Internal and External Training Programs
  • Study Visits under PEARL (JNNURM)
  • Coordinating Activities
  • Capacity Building for Urban Development (CBUD)
  • Project Studies and Reports
  • Government Programmes
  • Research and Development
  • Seminars and Workshops
  • Preparation of HRD and other related Manuals
  • Entries for Awards and Recognitions
