BALYAM - CHILDHOOD DEVELOPMENT PROGRAMME


It is a school readiness programme for 3 to 5 years age children. The Child is given freedom to do and to learn what he likes. As far as Balyam is concerned, "love is the language and play is the method,". Hence the Balyam should be called as the Second home of the Child. The programme was targeted to implement in all the 72 division of the city one per each ward and so far, 61 Centers were started.


A total No. of 61 Centers consisting 1963 children and functioning well in progress, each center accommodated by one Teacher and one Aaya. Out of Six Zones Five Supervisors are Working.


Admissions from Balyam to Primary Schools

  • 2009-2010 516 Students
  • 2010-2011 786 Students
  • 2011-2012 814 Students
  • 2012-2013 800 Students
  • 2013-2014 830 Students
  • 2014 – 15 : 567 Students
  • 2015 – 16 : 583 Students
  • 2016 – 17 : 580 Students
  • 2017 – 18 : 582 Students

The above Students were admitted into the First Standards in the respective G.V.M.C. Primary Schools”. This is a significant achievement which would not have been happened without these centers. So far, 3746 children have been enrolled in to our G.V.M.C. Primary Schools from G.V.M.C. Balyam Centers. There is ample evidence demonstrating the Quality Improvement at the Primary Level among the students who have had Pre School Education at Balyam Centers.
