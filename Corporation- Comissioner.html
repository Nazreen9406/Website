Daily Schedule Of the Commissioner

Daily visit to the call center and interacts with the public from 6:00 A.M. to 7:00 A.M.

Conducts review meetings with the concerned Officers through conference from 7:00 A.M. to 7:30 A.M.

The commissioner and all officers are available to the public all through the day on Mondays and also depending on the possibility.

Daily visit to one ward of each zone from 7:30 A.M. on rotation except on holidays and festivals.

If there is a need of emergency you can reach the commissioner in Dial your commissioner program every Monday by calling toll free number 180042500009 from 10:30 A.M. to 11:30 A.M.

You can send grievances through online registration, IVRS, and also record and leave a message. The commissioner verifies them daily and tries to sort them out.
