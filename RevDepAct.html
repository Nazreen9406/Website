INTRODUCTION

The Revenue Department of GVMC deals with revenue generation by way of Property Tax, Vacant Land Tax and its collection and dealing with Remunerative Enterprises, Water Charges etc. The Revenue Department is the heart of the GVMC and several developmental works are being taken up on collection of taxes & funds from time to time.

  • Remunerative Enterprises
  • Water Charges
