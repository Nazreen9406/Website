Within the government

The gains of technology can be applied equally to improve the systems within the government what with its various departments and wings depending on and feeding into each other. The time and energy it takes to process and send information between various sections within a department and between various departments severely impedes the decision making process. The Establishment at GVMC has greatly benefited from these efforts through the computerization of Employee Payrolls and other day to day activities like the intra-section movement of files and documents. Budget Monitoring is also done through the entire lifecycle from the sanction orders to the disbursement of funds for various projects undertaken by GVMC including the incorporation of the Taxation Structure. The entire system has been migrated to the Accrual based system of accounting in contrast the hitherto followed practice based on Cash based accounting principles
