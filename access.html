Access to Public Documents

Various Governments orders, Schemes, Programs, Annual Budgets, Gazette Notifications, Legislation materials like Ordinances, Bills, Examination results etc. This category would cover all those areas where the citizens need information, quick and accurate from the government. But presently get it with great difficulty or at private costs. This would also make citizens get information in Real Time without exorbitant communication costs.
